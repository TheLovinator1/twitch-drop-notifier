Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2023-12-20

What information do we collect?

Cloudflare

Our website is protected by Cloudflare for security and performance. Please refer to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for details on how Cloudflare handles data. We (Panso) can not access any data that Cloudflare collects except for the following metrics that are displayed in our Cloudflare dashboard:

  • Unique Visitors
  • Total requests
  • Percentage of requests that were cached
  • Total bandwidth used
  • Total data served
  • Data cached
  • Top Traffic per country

Sentry

We use Sentry to track errors on our website. Errors are stored for 30 days and then automatically deleted. Information that is collected includes:

  • The stack trace
  • Browser information (For example: Firefox 122.0)
  • User agent (For example: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 10.0; Win64; x64; rv:122.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/122.0)
  • Operating system information (For example: Windows >=10)
  • URL (For example: https://panso.se/about/)
  • Headers (For example: Accept-Language: en-US,en;q=0.5)

Purpose of Data Collection

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Log Files: To identify and address website errors and improve performance.
  • Cloudflare: For security and content delivery.
  • Sentry: To identify and address website errors.

None of the data we collect is used for marketing purposes or sold to third parties.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or tracking technologies on our website.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. However, please review the privacy policies of Cloudflare and Sentry for information on how they handle data.

User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your information. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us.

Retention Period

Log files may be retained for a limited time for debugging purposes. We do not retain personal information unless required for legal or business reasons.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated. Check the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document to see when it was last updated. We will notify you of any material changes.
You can also check the git commit history for changes to this document here.

Contact Information

For privacy concerns or questions, please contact us.
